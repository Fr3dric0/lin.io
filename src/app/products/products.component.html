Webapplications

Webapplications has become a collection-term for different types of websites.

Single-page-applications

A single-page-application, is a static website replicating dynamic websites. It behaves much like mobile and desktop applications, just in the browser.

This means we can load data from a database, include user-login and lots more, and at the same time keep the application fast and low-priced.

Typical products for a single-page-application is games, administration system, portfolio sites or website who needs to be fast.

Computer Management System

Interested in creating your own blog? Building a webshop? Or you just want to decide the layout or content of you website yourself? Then a Computer Management System (CMS) is what you will need.

A popular CMS framework is Wordpress, often used by blogs, newspapers or webshops. However, many also use frameworks like Drupal and ImpressPages, if you want more control of the system yourself.

Static websites

Typically a collection of static webpages, with little to no extra logic. Is best suited for people who just want a very simple portfolio website.

REST-api

A REST-api is an interface used by developers to implement data access and user login, between multiple applications.

Mobile- and single-page-applications, are typical consumers of REST-api. However, it has become widely popular between all fields of Software development, because of it's ease of use and scalability.
